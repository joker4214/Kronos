## Site build tiers (scaled by business size)

| Tier | Price | Fit | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter Site** | $900–$1,200 one-time | Solo store / startup | 1–5 pages, single custom design, mobile-responsive, on-brand |
| **Growth Site** | $1,800–$2,200 one-time | Small business | 5–10 pages, more custom sections/integrations (booking, email capture, product feeds) |
| **Full Custom Site** | $3,000–$3,500 one-time | Growing business | Multi-page, more complex functionality (e-commerce, multi-location, custom features) |

## Maintenance add-on (recurring)

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic Care** | $39–$59/mo | Updates, backups, uptime monitoring, minor text/image edits |
| **Full Care** | $99–$149/mo | Everything in Basic + security monitoring, monthly speed/performance check, priority support, one small content/design update included monthly |

## Positioning

Same "professional results at a price a solo store owner can justify" logic as the existing three packages — these numbers sit below typical market rates for each business-size tier (see research), keeping Dharma's undercut positioning consistent as the offer expands beyond content into full site builds.

## Research basis

- Small business site builds (freelancer): $1,500–$8,000 market range
- Small agency: $5,000–$25,000 · Mid-size agency: $15,000–$75,000 · Enterprise: $50,000–$300,000+
- Maintenance: $20–$500/mo market range, $75–$200/mo typical full-service for small business

Sources: [Levitate — Average Website Design Cost 2026](https://www.levitate.ai/blog-posts/average-website-design-cost-for-small-businesses-in-2026), [GruffyGoat — Small Business Website Costs 2026](https://gruffygoat.com/blog/small-business-website-cost), [WebCostEstimator — Web Design Agency Cost 2026](https://webcostestimator.com/guides/web-design-agency-cost/), [WP Super Help — Web Design Pricing](https://wpsuperhelp.com/web-design-pricing/), [FatLab — Website Maintenance Packages 2026](https://fatlabwebsupport.com/blog/website-maintenance/website-maintenance-packages-compared-your-complete-buyers-guide/), [websitemaintenanceservices.org — Cost Guide](https://websitemaintenanceservices.org/how-much-does-website-maintenance-cost/), [WebFX — Website Maintenance Pricing 2026](https://www.webfx.com/web-development/pricing/website-maintenance/)
