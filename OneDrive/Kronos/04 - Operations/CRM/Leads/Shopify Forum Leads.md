# Shopify Forum Leads — pet/pet-adjacent stores

*Sourced 2026-07-05 from Shopify's own Store Feedback community forum — these are store owners who publicly asked for help, so they're pre-qualified as both real (live stores) and actively wanting exactly what Dharma's sells. Reddit wasn't accessible to search from here, so this pulled from community.shopify.com instead.*

*Next step: run these through the Web Scraper checker (`04 - Operations/Web Scraper/scraper.py`) for the technical/SEO scoring, or just use the human feedback quoted below directly — the community already did a lot of that diagnostic work for us.*

## 1. Posh Pets

- **URL:** poshpetsstore.myshopify.com
- **Niche:** luxury pet products
- **Thread:** [Seeking Feedback on My Premium Pet Store](https://community.shopify.com/t/seeking-feedback-on-my-premium-pet-store-posh-pets/600729)
- **Self-reported problem:** wants to feel more "premium," asked about conversion/checkout friction
- **What the community found:** no custom domain, product schema shows a supplier's brand name instead of theirs (hurts search/AI visibility), pricing mix ($5 bandanas next to "luxury" positioning) undercuts the premium story, slow mobile speed
- **Outreach angle:** brand positioning + store optimization is a near-exact match for the Growth Bundle ($1,450) — GA4/SEO cleanup, product optimization, brand enhancement all directly address what the community flagged

## 2. CreativeSignet (pet art / dog portrait bundles)

- **URL:** creativesignet.com
- **Niche:** pet art, digital product bundles
- **Thread:** [Looking for Feedback on My Pet Art Shopify Store](https://community.shopify.com/t/looking-for-feedback-on-my-pet-art-shopify-store/565053)
- **Self-reported problem:** high bounce rate, CTR dropped from 5.5% to under 3%, traffic mostly outside target market (US), unsure if SEO setup is working
- **Outreach angle:** this is almost entirely a Starter Launch / SEO-optimization conversation — she's already trying (has an SEO app installed) but not seeing results, which is a good "let a pro look at it" opening

## 3. Petpala / Petpal Accessories (dog grooming/welfare)

- **URL:** petpalaccessories.com
- **Niche:** dog grooming and pet care products
- **Thread:** [6 Months, 0 Sales – Need Honest Feedback](https://community.shopify.com/t/6-months-0-sales-need-honest-feedback-on-my-dog-grooming-welfare-store/409751)
- **Self-reported problem:** 6 months running paid Facebook/Instagram ads, zero sales
- **What the community found:** weak homepage hierarchy/emotional appeal, product options in plain dropdowns instead of visual swatches, thin trust signals, no educational/SEO content
- **Outreach angle:** highest-urgency lead of the four — actively spending ad budget with zero return. Full Agency ($2,200) territory: brand enhancement, store audit, content, email setup all apply

## 4. Woof M-E-O-W

- **URL:** woofm-e-o-wco.myshopify.com
- **Niche:** pet-themed gifts/merch (mugs, tumblers)
- **Thread:** [Fellow Shop Owners: I'd Love Your Feedback](https://community.shopify.com/t/fellow-shop-owners-id-love-your-feedback-on-my-store/631143)
- **Self-reported problem:** very new store (posted June 2026), wants feedback on homepage clarity/trust/mobile
- **What the community found:** no custom domain yet (domain-lock issue mentioned), default free theme, AI-looking product art, no About/contact info, community rated it 3/10
- **Outreach angle:** earliest-stage lead — good fit for Starter Launch once she's ready to invest; may need a gentler/more educational approach since she pushed back defensively on harsh forum feedback
