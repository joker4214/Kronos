- No educational/blog content — missing SEO opportunity for organic traffic
- Site speed flagged as a likely factor (recommended technical/speed audit)

**Full Agency ($2,200)** — this is the most urgent lead of the four (actively burning ad spend with zero return for 6 months). Store performance audit, brand enhancement, product optimization, and a blog post all directly answer what the community diagnosed.

Highest urgency — open with the ad-spend-with-zero-sales problem directly (they're already paying to acquire traffic that doesn't convert, which is a clear, costly, provable issue) rather than general "let us help with content."
