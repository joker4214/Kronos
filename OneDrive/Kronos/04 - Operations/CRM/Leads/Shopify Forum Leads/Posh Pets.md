# Posh Pets — Prospect Data Sheet

**URL:** poshpetsstore.myshopify.com
**Niche:** Luxury pet products
**Source:** [Shopify Community — Seeking Feedback on My Premium Pet Store](https://community.shopify.com/t/seeking-feedback-on-my-premium-pet-store-posh-pets/600729) (posted April 2026)
**Contact:** Vanessa (founder)

## What she asked for

First impressions, product page quality, navigation/UX, checkout friction, whether the store reads as a genuine luxury brand.

## What the community found

- Still on the `.myshopify.com` subdomain — no custom domain
- Product schema shows a supplier's brand name ("Bark Fifth Avenue") instead of Posh Pets — actively hurts Google Shopping and AI-search visibility (ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity won't attribute products to her)
- Catalog/pricing mismatch: $5 bandanas and $17 clips sitting next to "curated luxury" copy undercuts the premium story
- Seasonal merch mistimed (July 4th product leading the homepage in late April)
- Brand voice split — logo/copy mention both dogs and cats, but catalog is 90%+ dog-only
- Slow mobile PageSpeed score (70s range)

## Recommended fit

**Growth Bundle ($1,450)** — GA4 setup, on-page SEO, and product optimization directly address the schema/visibility problem; brand enhancement (Full Agency tier) would also help resolve the positioning/catalog mismatch if she wants to go further.

## Suggested angle

Lead with the schema/AI-search visibility issue specifically — it's a concrete, provable problem ("your products are being credited to another brand in search") that's not just opinion, and it's the kind of technical issue a store owner usually can't see or fix herself.
