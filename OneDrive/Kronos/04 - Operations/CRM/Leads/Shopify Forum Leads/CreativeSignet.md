# CreativeSignet — Prospect Data Sheet

**URL:** creativesignet.com
**Niche:** Pet art / dog portrait bundles (digital products)
**Source:** [Shopify Community — Looking for Feedback on My Pet Art Shopify Store](https://community.shopify.com/t/looking-for-feedback-on-my-pet-art-shopify-store/565053) (posted September 2025)
**Contact:** Natalia (founder, small team — husband helps with technical side)

## What she asked for

Store design/UX trust, SEO setup review, traffic quality (wants more US-based pet owners), what's blocking conversions.

## Self-reported data

- Live ~2 months, ~500 sessions total, most traffic from outside the US (wrong audience)
- High bounce rate, unsure if it's design, pricing, or targeting
- Google Search Console CTR dropped from ~5.5% to under 3%
- Already has an SEO app installed (Tapita) and optimized meta/images — still ~70-80 PageSpeed score
- Only a few sales, all from direct traffic (not search or social)

## Community feedback

- Reviews section needs a redesign for trust/appeal
- Descriptions okay but SEO could use specialist attention
- Pricing questioned relative to competitors (Kittl, Canva, Envato) — needs clearer value communication in copy
- Running as essentially a solo operation — overwhelmed juggling listings, SEO, promotion, admin

## Recommended fit

**Starter Launch ($650)** as an entry point — she's stretched thin solo and already trying the DIY route without traction; content/ad copy + hashtag strategy could redirect toward the right audience (US pet owners) fast. On-page SEO à la carte ($100) or Growth Bundle if she wants GA4 + deeper optimization too.

## Suggested angle

She's already invested effort and money (SEO app) without results — the story isn't "you're doing it wrong," it's "you're doing the right things but missing the professional layer that makes it work." Matches the brand's "reframe as visibility/strategy, not quality" voice rule exactly.
