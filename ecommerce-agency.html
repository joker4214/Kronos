Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? ▼
Most projects take 6-10 weeks from kickoff to launch. The Starter package is usually 4-6 weeks. We build in phases so you can see progress every 2 weeks.
Do you work with platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom code? ▼
We work with all platforms. Most e-commerce clients use Shopify (it's faster and cheaper). We also build on WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or custom stacks. We recommend based on your specific needs.
What if I'm not happy with the design? ▼
We include 2 rounds of revisions during the design phase. We also do discovery calls upfront so we're aligned on direction before we build. Mismatches are rare.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch? ▼
Yes. Every package includes post-launch support (30-90 days). After that, we offer optional retainers for ongoing optimization, new features, and support.
Can you help us migrate from our current site? ▼
Yes. We handle migrations carefully—redirects, data transfer, SEO preservation, all of it. No downtime, no lost revenue.
What's your process for conversion optimization? ▼
We analyze heatmaps, user recordings, and your data. We identify friction points and test improvements. Small changes (copy, button color, form fields) often drive 10-25% improvement in conversions.
